University of Arizona, Tucson

Shot on: DJI Mavic Mini

Edited using: Adobe Lightroom

I think this is my favorite drone picture I've taken till now. If I remember correctly, I took this photo just 4 or 5 days after buying the drone. The building which you see in the middle is Old Main, and the large grass field in the back is the UA Mall. I was out with a couple of friends looking for photo opportunities when one of my friends took a picture from a slighly different angle than this. This inspired me take this picture. I think I like the way the lights are adding volume to the picture the most.
