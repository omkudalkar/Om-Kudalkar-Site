University Boulevard, Tucson

Shot on: DJI Mavic Mini

Edited using: Adobe Lightroom

This picture is a testimony to the beauty of Tucson sunsets. I've always said that the best sunsets are in Tucson. I took this picture with the intention to capture exaclty what I have. I wanted to get the shining CatTran rails with the silhoutte of the mountains in the back. I wish the sun would've aligned itself right above the mountains in the center. That would've really completed the picture.
